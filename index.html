John Gilbert

Transit door and window products designed for long service life and maximum reliability.

John Gilbert and Co. Ltd. was established in 1932 and is redefining standards for the design and manufacture of door and window systems needed to equip the modern public passenger vehicle fleet. We supply primarily into Australasian and Asian markets, with customers as far away as the U.K.

Both for large and small fleet operators John Gilbert & Co have been designing and building bus door solutions for 25 years and have supplied more than 3000 vehicle sets of doors
